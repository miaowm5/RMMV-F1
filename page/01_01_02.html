The system requirements for this software are as follows.

OS Windows 7/8/8.1/10 (32bit/64bit)
Mac OS X 10.10 or higher
Debian 8 or higher (March 21st, 2017 Update)
CPU Intel Core 2 Duo or equivalent or greater
RAM 2GB or more
HDD 2GB or more of free disk space
Graphics Card One with OpenGL support
Display Resolution of 1280Ã—768 or greater
